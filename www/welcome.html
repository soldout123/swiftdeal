On Android
Chrome browser · 1 tap
  1. Open the link in Chrome
  2. An "Install SwiftDeal" banner will pop up at the bottom
  3. Tap Install — the app icon goes to your home screen
  4. Open it like any other app. No Play Store required.
On iPhone
Safari browser · 3 taps
  1. Open the link in Safari (not Chrome)
  2. Tap the Share button at the bottom (square with arrow up)
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Tap Add. The icon appears on your home screen — tap to launch.
