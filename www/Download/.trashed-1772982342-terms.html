5. Payments, Fees & Currency

5.1 Fare Calculation

Ride fares are calculated using our "Suggested Price" formula, which considers:

  • Base fare for the service type
  • Distance traveled (per kilometer rate)
  • Estimated travel time (per minute rate)
  • Current demand and availability
  • Local market conditions

Users may negotiate fares with drivers, but the suggested price serves as a fair market reference.

5.2 Currency

Transactions are processed in the local currency based on your detected location:

  • Jamaica: Jamaican Dollar (JMD)
